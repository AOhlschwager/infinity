Mission Objectives

Accomplish more Classified Objectives than the adversary(1 Objective Point)

Kill more army points (2 Objective Points)

Have between 75 and 150 surviving army points (2 Objective Points)

Have between 151 and 250 surviving army points (3 Objective Points)

Have more than 250 surviving army points (4 Objective Points)

Classified

Each player has 3 Classified Objectives (1 Objective Point each)

Counterespionage: fulfilled objective cards with target symbol will cancel cards with cross symbol

Deployment

8 inch deployment zone

Troopers with deployment special skills must pass a PH -3 or be deployed anywhere in deployment zone. Only MODs will be applied to this roll

Special Rules

16 inch Biotechvore zone in each half ot the table

At end of each turn all troopers belonging to Active player must make a Damage 14 BTS roll

Troopers possessing the STR attribute must make two of these rolls

All troopers inside of the Biotechvore zone will be automatically killed at the end of the third Game Round

Retreat rules are not applied
