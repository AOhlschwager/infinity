Capture and Protect

Mission Objectives

Captured the Enemy Beacon at the end of the game (3 Objective points)

Captured the Enemy Beacon with you DataTracker at the End of the game (2 Objective Points)

Captured the Enemey Beacon in your own Deployment Zone at the end of the game (1 Objective Point)

Prevent the enemy from Capturing your Beacon at the end of the game (3 Objective Points)

Classified

Each Player has 1 Classified Objective (1 Objective Point)

Provisions:flower symbol will give 1 extra Objective Point)

Deployment

12 inch deployment zones

Beacons:(12,24) and (36,24)

Special Rules

Pick up beacon: base to base (trooper in null w/ beacon, beacon w/ no Enemy troopers in contact, or friendly trooper w/ beacon). No roll, short skill

Models can only carry 1 beacon unless they have baggage

Markers cannot carry beacons

Beacons are considered Captured as long as that player is the only one that has a troop in base contact with it

DataTracker

No HVT
